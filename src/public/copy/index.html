Ready to Copy?

Place your physical document on the printer tray before continuing. Follow the steps below to complete your copy job.

  1. 1
    Place your document Align the paper face-down on the glass or insert it into the feeder tray.
  2. 2
    Set print preferences Choose paper size, colour mode, orientation, and number of copies in the next screen.
  3. 3
    Insert coins & confirm On the Confirm & Pay screen, insert the required amount to start the copy job.

Your document stays on the tray until you confirm.
