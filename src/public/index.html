Please select an print method

How to Print

  1. Scan the QR code with your phone and upload a file.
  2. Select your file from the received list.
  3. Tap Continue to settings.
  4. Choose color mode, copies, orientation, paper size, and page range.
  5. Insert coins until your balance covers the total, then confirm.
  6. Collect your printed pages.

Troubleshooting

  • File not showing? Tap New session and upload again.
  • Balance too low? Insert more coins before confirming.

How to Copy

  1. Place your page face-down on the scanner glass.
  2. Tap Check Document and review the preview.
  3. If the preview is correct, tap Continue to Config.
  4. Choose copy settings, then continue to confirmation.
  5. Insert coins until the required amount is reached, then confirm.
  6. Collect your copies from the output tray.

Troubleshooting

  • No document detected? Reposition the page and tap Retry.
  • Preview looks wrong? Tap Check Document again.
  • Balance too low? Insert more coins before confirming.

How to Scan

  1. Choose scan source, color mode, and resolution.
  2. Place your document, then tap Scan Document.
  3. Review the preview. Tap Rescan if needed.
  4. Tap Get Soft Copy.
  5. Choose delivery: Wireless (QR) to scan a code on your phone, or USB Flash Drive to export to a USB stick.

Troubleshooting

  • Scanner unavailable? Check the cable/power and try again.
  • QR link expired? Tap Refresh QR Link.
  • No USB detected? Insert or reinsert the drive and tap Refresh USB.
